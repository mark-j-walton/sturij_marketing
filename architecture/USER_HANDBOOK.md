# Sturij Marketing Workspace — User Handbook

*A plain-English guide to using the workspace safely, day to day.*
Live at **marketing.sturij.com/app.html**

---

## 1. What this is (and the one big idea)

This is your **safe posting workspace**. It helps you reach the right Facebook groups with the right message — **without getting the account restricted.**

The single most important thing to understand: **the app never posts to Facebook.** *You* post, by hand. The app **prepares** everything (the message, which group, whether it's safe) and **keeps the record**. You copy, you paste, you post. That human step is deliberate — it's what keeps the account safe. Facebook bans automation; we never automate.

## 2. Signing in

- Go to **marketing.sturij.com/app.html**.
- **Sign in with Google** — only approved Sturij accounts get in (`contact@sturij.com` for real use; Mark's account for testing).
- If you land on a blank/empty page, you're not signed in — use the **Sign in** button.

## 3. The golden rules (why the app sometimes says "no")

These are your **ban-shield**. The app enforces them so you don't have to remember them:

| Rule | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **12 posts a day, max** | Never more than 12 posts across all groups in one day. |
| **14-day cooldown per group** | Once you post to a group, you can't post to it again for 14 days. |
| **Respect each group's rules** | Some groups only allow ads on certain days (e.g. Tuesdays). |
| **Vary your message** | Don't send the identical post to lots of groups. |
| **You post — never the app** | Copy-and-paste by hand, every time. |

If a **"Mark posted" button is greyed out**, it's one of these rules protecting you — usually the group is **in its 14-day cooldown**, or you've hit **12 for the day**. *(We're improving the app to always tell you exactly why and when you can next post.)*

## 4. Your daily flow (the important bit)

1. **Open the workspace** and sign in.
2. Go to **Plan** → see today's suggested groups (greens first, only what's safe today).
3. Pick a group → go to **Post**.
4. Read the **guard pills** at the top of the group (posts left today · cooldown status · OK-to-post). If it's clear, the buttons are live.
5. Choose one of the **3 message variations** → click **Copy**.
6. Click **Open group on Facebook** → paste your message → **post it yourself** on Facebook.
7. Come back and click **Mark posted** → this **logs** that you posted (it does *not* post anything — it just records it, so the guardrails keep counting).

That's the loop: **plan → copy → paste on Facebook → mark posted.**

## 5. The tabs

- **Dashboard** — the overview: how many groups, tier mix, top reach, and a ban-risk banner (how many posts you've got left today).
- **Groups** — all 273 groups. Search, filter by tier (green = best), see reach.
- **Plan** — today's safe suggestions (greens first, under the daily cap, rule-locked groups only on their day).
- **Post** — where you pick a group, grab a message variation, and mark it posted.
- **DNA** — Sturij's voice, strategy and tone. This is the "recipe" the messages are built from. Editable.
- **Safety** — your restriction history and the enforced limits, always visible.

## 6. Understanding "tiers" (green / amber / red)

- **Green** = your best groups (proven reach). Post here first.
- **Amber** = the default; worth posting, not yet measured.
- **Red** = low reach. Lower priority.
The Plan always puts **greens first**.

## 7. If something looks wrong

- A **greyed button** is almost always a guardrail (cooldown/cap) — not a fault.
- If the page is blank, you're signed out — sign in again.
- Anything genuinely broken or confusing → the **Support** page (coming) lets you log it, and it'll be looked at.
