# Sturij Marketing — User Handbook

A plain-English guide to running the Sturij Facebook-marketing workspace. It is written for **Polly**, who does the day-to-day posting, and for **Mark**, who owns the account and the data.

The app lives at **marketing.sturij.com**. Sign in with the approved Google account. If sign-in is refused, your email isn't on the approved list — ask Mark.

---

## The one rule that governs everything

> **The app prepares. You post.**

The app drafts copy, assembles adverts, keeps you inside the safe limits and lays everything out ready. **The actual paste into Facebook is always done by a human — you.** The app has *no* connection to Facebook, never logs in, never posts, never schedules a real Facebook post. Every "Posted ✓" button in the app is you telling the app *"I've pasted this"* — it only writes a record on our side.

Why: Facebook bans accounts that behave like bots. Everything here is built to keep the Sturij account safe — daily caps, cooldowns, per-group rules. Trust the guardrails; they exist to protect the account.

---

## The safety limits (memorise these)

| Limit | What it means |
|---|---|
| **12 posts per day** | Never post to more than 12 groups in a day. The app blocks the 13th. |
| **14-day cooldown per group** | After you post to a group, don't post there again for 14 days. The app hides that group until it's safe. |
| **Group rules / posting days** | Some groups only allow promo on certain days (e.g. "Tuesdays only"). The app only offers those groups on their allowed day. |
| **Cleared photos only** | A photo can only be used in an advert once a person has *cleared it for public use*. Uncleared photos are locked out everywhere. |

You never have to track these yourself — the app does. But knowing them helps you understand *why* a group is greyed out or missing.

## The tabs, top to bottom

The left rail has these areas. Here's what each is for.

### Dashboard
Your morning glance. Shows how many groups are tracked, how many have real reach data, the green/amber/red tier split, **how many posts you have left today**, and days since the last account restriction. Below that: your **top-reach green groups**, your **top adverts**, and a date-range list of **previous adverts**.

### Groups
Every Facebook group Sturij targets (273 of them), grouped into **Region** or **Town** accordions. Each accordion header shows how many groups it holds, the tier split, and a red **⚠ no link** count. Inside, click any group to open its **detail drawer**:
- **Tier** (green / amber / red) — how safe/valuable the group is. Change it with one click.
- **Facebook reach** — views, viewers, impressions, engagement (where measured).
- **Group info** — members/size, posting note, avoid-topics, context, tags, notes. Hit **✎ Edit group info** to change any of it, or to **Archive** the group.
- **Posting days** — set which days you're allowed to post, or mark "Any day".
- **Facebook group link** — confirm a suggested link, or paste the real one.

A red **no link** chip means we don't yet have that group's Facebook URL (see *Audit* to add links in bulk).

### Audit
The **self-emptying worklist**. It shows *only* groups missing posting-critical data — a **link** (so you can reach the group) and a **posting rule** (so it's safe). Fill both and the group drops off the list.
- 🔴 **red** = no link (can't reach it — critical)
- 🟠 **amber** = has a link, no posting rule
- **Bulk import** — the fast way to add links: open Facebook's *"Groups you've joined"* list, copy it, and paste the whole block into the box. The app matches names to your groups and shows you a review table. **Nothing is saved until you confirm.** No Facebook automation is involved — you're pasting a list you already have.
- **Suggested links** — for some groups the app has suggested a link (found by searching the group's name). Open it, check it's the right group, and **Confirm**.

> Right now **245 of 273 groups have no link.** Clearing them via Bulk import is the single biggest data job before a full run.

### Plan
Your posting plan, in two parts.

**Top — Suggestions.** Greens first, under the 12/day cap, only groups safe today. Each has an **Add to Run** button that drops the job into your queue. (There's also a *This week* view showing eligible counts per day.)

**Bottom — Ready to post (your queue).** Everything you've added, **grouped by day** in date accordions. This is where you actually work a post:
1. **Draft the copy** — press **✨ Draft with AI** (or write your own). Copy saves automatically. **↻ Remix** regenerates it.
2. **⇈ Paste window** — opens a small **floating window that stays on top of Facebook**. It holds the copy, a one-tap **Copy** button, the **Open group ↗** link, and a **Posted ✓** button. Keep it floating over your browser while you paste.
3. **Open group ↗** — jumps to the Facebook group.
4. Paste the copy into Facebook by hand, post it.
5. **Posted ✓** — press it (on the card or in the paste window). This logs the post on our side and clears the job from the queue.

If a group isn't eligible today (cooldown or wrong day), **Posted ✓** is disabled with the reason shown — you can still prep the copy ahead of time.

### Run ("Today's Run")
An alternative, focused **one-group-at-a-time** posting flow — a guided queue that walks you through today's safe posts. Each step shows three AI draft options (lock the ones you like, remix the rest, pick one), a **Single post / A-B test** toggle, a live **Facebook-style preview**, the group link and its rules, and **Posted ✓ — next**. Same guardrails, same "you paste it" rule. Use whichever you prefer — Plan's queue or the Run stepper.

- **A/B test** outputs two variants (A and B) for Facebook's own A/B tool. It counts as **one** post against the cap, not two.

### Post
The **single-group cockpit** for one-off posts. Build the post at the top — pick or create an image, let AI draft the copy — then browse to any group (by region, town, top-10, or "not posted in N months"), open it, paste, and log. Same guarded write path.

### Photos
Your private image library. **Photos ⇄ Adverts** switch at the top.
- **Upload** — drag in JPG/PNG/WebP (up to 15 MB). **At least one tag is required** — tags are how you find images later. A thumbnail is made in your browser; the **original is stored privately** (only signed, expiring links — never public URLs).
- **Clear for public** — before an image can be used anywhere, someone must tick the 4-point checklist (no faces without consent, no valuables/address on show, OK to show publicly). **Only a person clears a photo — never the AI.** You can revoke clearance any time.
- **Find** — Recent & most-used by default; search by tag/description; filter by **By tag**, **Needs clearing**, **Cleared**. Long lists **paginate** (show 10/20/30/40/50).
- **Adverts** view — the montages you've built (see Montage), searchable, with view / edit / download / schedule / delete.

### Montage
Build an **advert** from cleared photos.
- Pick a **template** (layout). Built-ins: 3×3 square, 3×3 portrait, 2×2 landscape, single. Or press **+ New template** to build your own: set width×height, **pull divider lines** from the top and side rulers to carve the cells, name it and save.
- Click a cell → pick a cleared photo → **pan and zoom** it to frame (images are never stretched). **Lock** a cell to protect it; **Remix** reshuffles only the unlocked cells.
- **Download PNG** (full resolution) or **Save advert** (kept in your library, tags inherited from the photos). Saved adverts can be **scheduled** to a group.

### Themes
Seasonal / campaign steering for the AI writer (e.g. "Autumn kitchens", "New Year home offices"). Add a theme with a category, sub-category and description; when it's active the copy drafts lean into it.

### DNA
The brand's voice and strategy — the raw material the AI writer is grounded in. Four areas: **Voice & Personality, Strategy, Learning, Reference.** Edit any entry; it saves live and immediately shapes future drafts. This is how you make the copy sound like Sturij and not like a generic advert.

### Safety
The account's protection record. Shows **days since the last restriction**, a log of any Facebook restrictions/appeals, and your **Archived groups** (with a **Restore** button). Archiving hides a group from every list without deleting anything.

### Support
Raise a **bug, feedback, feature idea or backlog** item. Each ticket is auto-**triaged by AI** (severity, summary, likely cause) on creation. Filter by status/kind; close a ticket with a short plain-English resolution note. Use this throughout UAT — it's how every issue gets captured.

## How the AI helps (and its limits)

The AI **writes copy** — grounded in your DNA, the group's context and tone, the active theme, and (where given) the advert image. It drafts, remixes, and can work in a light or bold tone. That's preparation, which is allowed.

The AI **never**:
- posts to Facebook or touches Facebook in any way,
- clears a photo for public use (only a person does that),
- decides to break a cap, cooldown or group rule.

Group text, ticket text and notes are treated as **information, not instructions** — so a stray line in a group's rules can't hijack the AI.

## A good daily routine

1. **Dashboard** — check posts left today and any restriction warning.
2. **Plan** — look at today's suggestions; **Add to Run** the ones you want.
3. **Ready to post** — for each queued job: **✨ Draft**, tidy the copy, open the **⇈ Paste window**, **Open group ↗**, paste, post on Facebook, **Posted ✓**.
4. Stop when the day's cap is reached or you've cleared the queue.
5. Anything odd → **Support**, raise a ticket.

**Filling data (as you go):** whenever you touch a group with no link or no rule, add them there and then — it makes tomorrow's run better.

## Troubleshooting

| You see… | It means… | Do this |
|---|---|---|
| A group greyed out / "Planning only" | It's in cooldown or off its posting day | Prep the copy; post when eligible (date shown) |
| "12/12 — cap reached" | You've hit today's safe limit | Stop for the day; protect the account |
| Red **no link** chip | We don't have that group's Facebook URL | Add it in the group drawer or Audit → Bulk import |
| Photo won't appear in Montage | It isn't cleared for public | Clear it in Photos (4-point checklist) |
| The floating paste window doesn't stay on top | Your browser isn't Chrome/Edge | Use Chrome or Edge for the always-on-top window; elsewhere it opens as a normal pop-out |
| "Allow pop-ups…" | The browser blocked the paste window | Allow pop-ups for marketing.sturij.com |
| Sign-in refused | Your email isn't approved | Ask Mark to approve it |

- **Something broken or confusing** → raise it in **Support** (that's what UAT is for).

- **Account restricted by Facebook** → tell Mark immediately; log it in Safety.
